About The Golden Roll

The Golden Roll was created with one simple vision: to celebrate and elevate Filipino heritage through food.

Inspired by the traditional Filipino delicacy Daral, our brand brings a modern twist to a beloved local snack while honoring its cultural roots.

By combining traditional ingredients with modern flavors, we transform a classic treat into something exciting for today's generation.

What is Daral?

Daral is a traditional Filipino dessert made from a thin crepe rolled and filled with sweet coconut.

It originated from the Tausug people of the Sulu Archipelago and is known for its delicate wrapper made from glutinous rice and coconut milk.
