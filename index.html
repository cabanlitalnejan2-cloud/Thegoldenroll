Our Story

The Golden Roll was created with one simple vision: to celebrate and elevate Filipino heritage through food. Inspired by the traditional Filipino delicacy Daral, our brand brings a modern twist to a beloved local snack. While many Filipino delicacies remain rooted in tradition, we believe they can also evolve with creativity and innovation while still honoring their cultural origins.

Our goal is to introduce Daral to a wider audience and allow more Filipinos to rediscover the beauty of local delicacies. By combining traditional ingredients with modern flavors, we transform a classic treat into something exciting, fresh, and accessible for today's generation.

At The Golden Roll, every bite represents a connection between Filipino roots and modern taste. We believe local delicacies deserve to be appreciated not only as traditional foods, but as innovative culinary experiences that proudly showcase Filipino culture.

Through this project, we aim to inspire more people to support, appreciate, and celebrate Filipino products.

What is Daral?

Daral is a traditional Filipino dessert made from a thin crepe rolled into a cylindrical shape and filled with sweetened coconut. It is a delicacy that originated from the Tausug people of the Sulu Archipelago in the southern Philippines.

The outer wrapper of Daral is similar to a lumpia wrapper, but it is traditionally made from a batter of ground glutinous rice mixed with coconut milk, giving it a soft and delicate texture.

Inside the roll is a sweet filling made from shredded coconut cooked with sugar, creating a rich and flavorful coconut filling known as hinti.

Daral is commonly enjoyed as a snack or dessert and is known for its simple ingredients, delicate texture, and naturally sweet coconut flavor.

Our Modern Take

The Golden Roll transforms the traditional Daral into a modern Filipino snack experience. While we preserve the authentic coconut filling that makes Daral special, we also introduce new and exciting flavor variations designed to appeal to modern tastes.

By blending tradition with innovation, we aim to make Daral more accessible and appreciated by the new generation of Filipino snack lovers.
