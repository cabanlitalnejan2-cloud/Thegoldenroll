Our Story

The Golden Roll was created with one simple vision: to celebrate and elevate Filipino heritage through food. Inspired by the traditional Filipino delicacy Daral, our brand brings a modern twist to a beloved local snack.

While many Filipino delicacies remain rooted in tradition, we believe they can also evolve with creativity and innovation while still honoring their cultural origins.

Our goal is to introduce Daral to a wider audience and allow more Filipinos to rediscover the beauty of local delicacies.


What is Daral?

Daral is a traditional Filipino dessert made from a thin crepe rolled into a cylindrical shape and filled with sweetened coconut.

It originated from the Tausug people of the Sulu Archipelago.

The wrapper is made from glutinous rice batter mixed with coconut milk while the filling is shredded coconut cooked with sugar called hinti.
